- To restart apache: sudo /etc/init.d/apache2 restart
- Config files: /etc/apache2/apache2.conf; other config files in same folder
- fastcgi + nginx: http://www.eschrade.com/page/why-is-fastcgi-w-nginx-so-much-faster-than-apache-w-mod_php/
- To enable modules in apache: a2enmod: a2enmod rewrite, a2enmod userdir; a2dismod to disable
- Similarly, to enable sites in sites-available: a2ensite, a2dissite
